Substantial Presence Test

U.S. Tax Residency Tool for 2024

Note on Partial Days: Count every day you were physically present in the U.S. for any part of a day, including arrival and departure days.

IRS Rule: Students are typically "Exempt Individuals" for 5 years; Teachers for 2 years.

💡

Closer Connection Exception

You meet the mathematical test, but because you spent less than 183 days in the U.S. this year, you might still be treated as a Non-Resident if you can prove a closer connection to another country via Form 8840.
